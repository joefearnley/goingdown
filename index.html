Visualizing my weightloss

After a failed attempt in 2012, I gave the Slow Carb Diet another shot. So far it is working. I ended up losing 20lbs in 2 months.

The data is being tracked from my phone using WeightBot. I built a backend that retrieves the data from wieghtbot.com and serves it up on heroku.
